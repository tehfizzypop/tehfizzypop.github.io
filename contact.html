Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our property management services.

What services do you provide?

We provide comprehensive property management services including tenant screening, rent collection, property maintenance, financial reporting, and 24/7 emergency support for both residential and commercial properties.

How much do your services cost?

Our fees are competitive and transparent, typically ranging from 8-12% of monthly rent depending on the services required and property type. We offer customized packages to meet your specific needs.

How quickly do you respond to maintenance requests?

We prioritize maintenance requests based on urgency. Emergency issues are addressed immediately, while routine maintenance is typically completed within 24-48 hours. We keep tenants updated throughout the process.

Do you handle both residential and commercial properties?

Yes, we manage both residential and commercial properties. Our team has extensive experience with single-family homes, multi-unit buildings, office spaces, retail locations, and industrial properties.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the greater metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs. Contact us to confirm if we manage properties in your specific location, as our service area is continuously expanding.

How do I get started with your services?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact us through our website, phone, or email. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your needs, provide a customized proposal, and answer any questions you may have.
